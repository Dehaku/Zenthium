After every combat encounter or mission completion, the kills, incaps, healing performed, stealth takedowns, collateral damage, and 
looting will be talleyed.
-Additional Tracking; Failed Stealth maneuvers(Being detected)
This information will then be filtered through your faction's ethics of secrecy and manipulation, then applied to your populations.
Population who dislike violence will be upset with kills...
Lawful population will be unhappy with looting...
Pacifist population will be happy with incaps and healing...
Secretive population will be unhappy with failed stealth, but happy with stealth takedowns.

Additionally, mission completions can be willingly broadcasted to enhance the effects on your population, but will also 
 affect neighboring factions
